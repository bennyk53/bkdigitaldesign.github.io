Logo Design

Below are logos that I have created for businesses of all kinds.



This logo was created for Steel Consulting. Steel Consulting is a consulting company aimed specifically at agricultural businesses offering various services to their customers that allow them to grow their business. Steel Consulting wanted a logo that had a steel look, as well as a logo that looked strong, timeless, modern and up to date. They wanted to convey that they are a company that are up to date with everything that is going on, a strong company that is reliable, and will stand the test of time.

Even in the world of vintage clothing there is a need for new technology and design. I worked with Dugout Vintage, a vintage clothing store in London, Ontario, Canada, to produce their new logo. They had a previous logo, but they wanted something new and something that did a better job of reflecting who they were. We worked together trying out a large number of different designs and ideas using the colour scheme that they provided until we came to this design. This logo allows them to continue to grow with a more flexible, and defining logo while still being connected to the origins of the company and the supporters that they have accumulated over the years.
